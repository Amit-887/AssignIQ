# 🎓 Assignment System - Complete Guide

## 🚀 How to Use the New Assignment Features

### 📋 **For Teachers**

#### **1. Create Assignments**
- Go to **Dashboard** → **My Sections** → Select a section
- Click **"Create Assignment"**
- Fill in:
  - Title & Description
  - Due Date & Max Marks
  - Enable AI features (recommended)
  - Allowed file types (PDF, DOC, Images, etc.)

#### **2. View Student Submissions**
- Navigate to **Assignments** tab in your section
- Click on any assignment to view:
  - 📊 **Performance Analytics** (average scores, originality)
  - 📁 **All Student Submissions** with files
  - 🎯 **Individual Performance** (9/10 format)
  - 🤖 **AI Analysis Results**

#### **3. Review Student Work**
- Click **👁️ View Details** on any submission
- See:
  - Uploaded files (downloadable)
  - AI-generated questions & student answers
  - Plagiarism & originality scores
  - Performance feedback

---

### 👨‍🎓 **For Students**

#### **1. Access Assignments**
- Go to **Dashboard** → **My Sections** → Select your section
- Click **"View Assignments"**
- See all available assignments with due dates

#### **2. Submit Assignment (Step-by-Step)**

**Step 1: Upload Files & Content**
- 📁 **Upload Files**: PDF, DOC, DOCX, TXT, or images
- 📱 **Mobile Photo**: Click "Take Photo" to capture documents
- 📝 **Add Content**: Type or paste your assignment text

**Step 2: AI Precheck (Recommended)**
- Click **"Precheck with AI"** before submitting
- Get instant feedback on:
  - ✅ **Originality Score** (higher is better)
  - ⚠️ **Plagiarism Risk** (lower is better)
  - 🤖 **AI Content Detection**
  - 💡 **Improvement Suggestions**

**Step 3: Submit Assignment**
- After fixing issues from precheck, click **"Submit Assignment"**
- Files are uploaded and processed

**Step 4: Answer AI Questions**
- System generates questions based on your content
- Answer to show your understanding
- Get scored on comprehension (part of final grade)

#### **3. View Your Performance**
- Go to **"My Performance"** tab
- See:
  - 📊 **Overall Statistics** (average score, on-time submissions)
  - 📈 **Recent Submissions** with scores
  - 🎯 **Real-time Updates** as teacher reviews

### 🤖 **AI Features**

#### **Precheck Analysis**
- **Originality**: How unique is your content? (0-100%)
- **Plagiarism Risk**: Similarity to existing sources (0-100%)
- **AI Content**: How much was AI-generated? (0-100%)
- **Suggestions**: Specific improvement recommendations
- **Issues**: Problems to fix (low/medium/high severity)

#### **Smart Questions**
- AI generates 5 questions based on your content
- Tests understanding of key concepts
- Scores your answers automatically
- Contributes to final performance score

#### **Performance Scoring**
- **9/10 Format**: Easy-to-understand scoring
- **Real-time Updates**: See scores immediately
- **Detailed Feedback**: Know what you did well/poorly

---

### 🎯 **Performance Tracking**

#### **For Students**
- **Overall Average**: All assignments combined
- **Individual Scores**: Each assignment breakdown
- **Originality Metrics**: Content quality tracking
- **On-time Rate**: Punctuality statistics

#### **For Teachers**

- **Score Distribution**: How many students excelled/struggled
- **Quality Metrics**: Class originality & plagiarism averages

### 📍 **Navigation Paths**

2. **Direct URL**: `/assignments/:sectionId` (teacher view)

#### **Students:**
1. **Login** → **Dashboard** → **My Sections** → Select Section → **View Assignments**
2. **Direct URL**: `/assignments/:sectionId` (student view)

#### **Performance Tracking:**
- **Students**: Click "My Performance" tab in assignments
